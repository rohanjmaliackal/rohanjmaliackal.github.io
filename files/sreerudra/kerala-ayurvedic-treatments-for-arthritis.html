Types and Ayurvedic Treatments for Arthritis
Arthritis is one of the common disease that occur among people world wide.The term arthritis is used for almost a group of 100 diseases.And it bear some common Characteristics like inflammation and pain in joints.Arthritis can range from mild achesto chronic pain. There is currently no cure for arthritis. But ayurveda give a natural healing and relief from chronic pain caused by this undesired disease. There are many types of arthritis but generally 3 types are considered

Rheumatoid Arthritis
It is an autoimmune disease that can lead to the joint inflammation.It is a painful condition which causes deformity in joints.In Ayurveda, it is known as Aamvata which happens due to thevata vitiation in the body.

Osteoarthritis
In this kind of arthritis, the cartilaginous tissue in the joints get converted to bony structures.Due to this , the movements of joints get restricted and cause severe pain in moving joints. In ayurveda, it is known as sandhivata and is caused due to vitiation in the body.

Gout
This disease is more commonly known as riches diseases as rich people are commonly seen suffering from this kind of ailment.This type of arthritis occurs due to lifestyle disorders and seen commonly in people who lacks physical activities in their life.In Gout,due to crystal formation of uric acid in the joints hinders the free movement of the joint. In ayurveda, this situation is called Vata Rakta.

Kerala is well- known for its ayurveda treatments and ayurvedic centres.Ayurvedic medicines has found better results and marked improvements in pain relief and the severity of swollen joints. Different Kerala Ayurvedic centers are popular for different treatments. According to Ayurveda, the joints, tendons and cartilage involved in arthritis are do not exist in separation from the rest of the body and ayurveda concentrates on the body as a whole, everything from head to toe, physically and emotionally rather than concentrating on the particular joints affected by arthritis. First you must start with a basic of eating the right food for your body to achieve a stable and healthy body. Another vital matter is calming and relaxing your mind and body by doing meditation or variety of ayurvedic meditation.
