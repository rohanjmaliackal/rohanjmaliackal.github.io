ABOUT SMP TRADING CORPORATION

Welcome To SMP TRADING CORPORATION

SMP trading is one of the best and leading plywood and particle board manufacturers in south India. We have been established in both India and abroad being engaged in producing and marketing high quality products. We have an excellent track record for quality of products and services. The timbers for producing the plywood are imported from the best timber available places like Africa, New Zealand, Burma and Malaysia. The selected timbers are gone through various processes to enhance durability and to improve quality through latest technology advanced machineries. We can ensure the quality and durability of products for any of the carpentry works to suit your requirements.Inorder to cater the various requirements of clients, we provide products in different sizes, colors, shapes and designs as per their necessities .The best part of SMP trading is the availability of variety plywood types- BWP 710 grade, BWR 303 grade, MR grade plywood, Film coated shuttering plywood, Block boards, Flush doors and a variety of design options. Due to our quality products and ethical business , we have successfully become a prime choice of our valued clients. We have mustered huge clients across the globe.
