about


Wedding bells photography is the brain child of Aswin and Sreenath and was born on October 3, 2014. We are a bunch of hyper enthusiastic devoted people who believe in the power of photograph. As passion turned professionals; we go to any extent for that perfect composition.
We believe that emotions are the life of a photograph. Each photo should make you remember the best memories of that day. A combination of beautiful light, beautiful location, a dynamic composition and the well-timed ‘click’ gives you photos that speak.

pre wedding photography

With Pre wedding shoots, couple wants to cherish beautiful moments between them before wedlock. They want to announce their commitment towards each other. Some book pre wed shoots to overcome camera fear and improve chemistry between them. Pre wedding shoots help you two to get to know each other more.

post wedding photography

Bonding between newlyweds actually begins after they are relieved from the busy wedding schedules. The hesitancy is replaced with tranquil serenity. Indulge in beautiful hours of togetherness (in front of the camera). The stress of the big day is gone and presents a perfect scenario for gorgeous photos.

maternity photography

Preserve the memories of your journey towards parenthood with wedding bells photography. Maternity photo-shoots involve the husband and the first born child which creates a lively moment for the expectant mother. These shoots are a great experience for an expectant mother who undergoes emotional and physical stress during this period.

candid wedding photography

Candid photography involves taking pictures of people without their cognizance. To capture someone in action is one of the beauties of photography. It adds life. It is all about capturing unplanned moments in a planned shoot whether it is cheers or tears.

north indian wedding

North Indian weddings are a spectacular and luxurious ceremony in all of India. The celebrations and customs are vibrant and variant each day. From Sagai to Bidayi it is a festive mood for everyone. Apart from the riches, these ceremonies boast off a great deal of rituals and ceremonies.

explore

south indian wedding

South Indian weddings are no exception from North Indian celebrations but are very different from them. Here, each ritual is symbolic of lovely and gracious sentiments. South Indian weddings stick on to an age old customs rather than a lavish affair. These weddings are fun- filled with light moments to cherish.

explore

celebrity photography

Celerity weddings are a venue for lavish affairs and get together for prominent people. They are large ceremonies with full of highly important people. Wedding Bells photography have the flair to manage such big events with ease.
